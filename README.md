# School Business Brief Generator

This APM skill helps educators, administrators, and school leaders create professional business briefs for educational initiatives. Whether you're proposing a new after-school program to the school board, pitching a curriculum update to the principal, or seeking grant funding for a student support service, this skill structures your ideas into a clear, persuasive document.

The skill generates comprehensive briefs with executive summaries, stakeholder impact analyses, resource requirements, implementation timelines, and success metrics—all tailored to the unique context of schools and educational institutions. It understands the difference between K-12 and higher education environments and adjusts recommendations accordingly.

Use this skill to save time on documentation, ensure you've addressed critical decision-making criteria, and communicate your vision effectively to busy administrators and board members.
